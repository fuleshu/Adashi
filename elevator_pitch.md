#Elevator pitch
Basically adashi should help vibe coders to automatically make their projects professional guided, structured, so the ai can implement it efficiently and keep track and organize huge projects, not only small one shot prompts. It also makes it more transparent, so the user understands easily what the ai has build.

#Workflow
The professional workflow should be:
-The user describes the product.
-AI creates and validates the architecture.
-AI decomposes it into dependency-ordered tasks.
-AI executes each task with its linked design context.
-AI records completion evidence.
-AI creates and runs linked QA.
-Adashi exposes gaps, stale work, dependencies, progress, and verification status.
